How to Play

Find groups of 4 related law school concepts. Each group shares a connection.

  • Tap tiles to select them
  • Select 4 tiles and tap "Submit"
  • If correct, you'll reveal the category
  • If wrong, you lose a life (4 max)
  • Identify all 4 groups to complete the puzzle

Difficulty increases with each group: Yellow (easiest) → Green → Blue → Purple (hardest)
