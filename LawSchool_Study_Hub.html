Game → Exam Skill Map

🐝 Issue Spotter Bee → Issue Spotting

The single most exam-relevant game. Trains you to see facts and instantly think "what doctrines apply?" — exactly what professors test. Target: Genius on 60%+ of puzzles.

🟪 Connections → Categorization

Exams reward precise mental categories. Connections forces you to distinguish similar-looking doctrines. Target: Solving blue/purple without mistakes.

🟩 Rule Wordle → Element Recall

Professors give full credit for complete, precise rule statements. Wordle trains you to recall every element. Target: Completing in 3 or fewer guesses.

📜 Case Quotes → Case Attribution

You can't write "the wheat case" — you need "Wickard v. Filburn." Case Quotes trains instant case-name recall. Target: Guessing in 1-2 tries.

🔗 Strands → Cross-Topic Synthesis

A+ exams connect doctrines across topics. Strands trains you to see how adverse possession links to easements links to recording acts. Target: Finding the Spangram without hints.

⬛ Crossword → Terminology Speed

Quick recall of terms and case names during a 3-hour exam. Daily crossword keeps your vocabulary sharp. Target: Beating your personal best time.
